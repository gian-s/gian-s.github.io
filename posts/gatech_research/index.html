Deep Learning for Autism Detection

Deep Learning models to detect when questions are being asked given an audio file for use in Autism detection
July 2021 · Georgia Institute of Technology | Suggest Changes

During an Undergraduate Research Program, I worked alongside Professor David V. Anderson, and PhD mentor Desmond Caulley on developing deep learning models to assist speech pathologist accurately diagnose Autism Spectrum Disorder.

The motivations behind the work and the results I got from my experiments this summer can be found in this power point, which I presented during the final presentations at powerpoint portion of Georgia Tech’s Summer Undergraduate Research in Engineering/Sciences (SURE) program. My presentation and findings won me a 1st place prize amongst the other computing sciences researchers.
