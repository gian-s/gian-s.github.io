City of Revere Data Analysis

Exploratory data analysis on whether or not racial disparities are present in the use of city services such as garbage collection, tracking violations, housing violation, etc.
May 2021 · City of Revere | Suggest Changes

Overarching semester project worked on a team of 6 for Computational Tools for Data Science at Boston University CS506. Our semester long project was working with city offcials in Revere, Massachusetts in order to track the use of city services by demographics. Our tasks included working with geo-spatial data in order to city-wide data by census tracts. This was done doing spatial merging using coordindates given by each

The motivations behind the work and the results I got from my experiments this summer can be found in the power point link.
